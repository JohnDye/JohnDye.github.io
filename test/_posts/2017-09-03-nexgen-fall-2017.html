Bridgeway Church Next Generation (NexGen) Kids Fall 2017

✻

Our Motto: "Partnering with parents to disciple the next generation".
Curriculum: The Gospel Project


The Gospel Project® for Kids immerses all of our K-12 children in the gospel through every story, theological concept, and call to mission from Genesis to Revelation.

Purpose of This Blog

Each week, utilizing the information provided below, it is our hope that parents will:

  • Assist their child in memorizing the current unit's memory verse.
  • Ask your child for the answer to the current unit's Big Picture Question.
  • Discuss each week's lesson with your child.
  • The Goal: To help your child better understand, retain, and apply God's word in their life.

UNIT 25: JESUS THE ANOINTED ONE

Memory verse: Luke 4:18 The Spirit of the Lord is upon me, because he has anointed me to proclaim good news to the poor. He has sent me to proclaim liberty to the captives and recovering of sight to the blind, to set at liberty those who are oppressed, to proclaim the year of the Lord's favor.

Big Picture Question: Why do believers share the Lord's Supper?
Big Picture Answer: Believers share the Lord's Supper to remember Jesus' life and death and to proclaim him until he returns.

UNIT 26: JESUS THE SAVIOR

Memory verse: Ephesians 2:8-9 For by grace you have been saved through faith. And this is not your own doing; it is the gift of God, not a result of works, so that no one may boast.

Big Picture Question: Why did Jesus die on the cross?
Big Picture Answer: Jesus died on the cross to save us from our sins and came back to life to show we are forgiven.

UNIT 27: JESUS THE RISEN KING

Memory verse: 1 Corinthians 15:3-4 For I delivered to you as of first importance what I also received: that Christ died for our sins in accordance with the Scriptures, that he was buried, that he was raised on the third day in accordance with the Scriptures.

Big Picture Question: What is our mission as believers?
Big Picture Answer: Our mission as believers is to make disciples of all nations by the power of the Spirit.
