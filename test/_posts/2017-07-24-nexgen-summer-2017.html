Bridgeway Church Next Generation (NexGen) Kids Summer 2017

✻

Our Motto: "Partnering with parents to disciple the next generation".
Curriculum: The Gospel Project


The Gospel Project® for Kids immerses all of our K-12 children in the gospel through every story, theological concept, and call to mission from Genesis to Revelation.

Purpose of This Blog

Each week, utilizing the information provided below, it is our hope that parents will:

  • Assist their child in memorizing the current unit's memory verse.
  • Ask your child for the answer to the current unit's Big Picture Question.
  • Discuss each week's lesson with your child.
  • The Goal: To help your child better understand, retain, and apply God's word in their life.

UNIT 22: JESUS THE STORYTELLER

Memory verse: Mark 6:34 When Jesus landed and saw a large crowd, he had compassion on them, because they were like sheep without a shepherd. So he began teaching them many things.

Big Picture Question: Why did Jesus tell parables?
Big Picture Answer: Jesus told parables to teach people about God and His kingdom.

UNIT 23: JESUS THE MIRACLE WORKER

Memory verse: John 20:31 But these are written that you may believe that Jesus is the Messiah, the Son of God, and that by believing you may have life in his name.

Big Picture Answer: Jesus performed miracles to glorify God and prove He is God the Son.

Memory verse: Psalm 103:2-3 Praise the Lord, my soul, and forget not all His benefits - who forgives all your sins and heals all your diseases ...
