We are excited that Mike Mckee will speak at Bridgeway on July 9. Mike was a part of the Summit Church Planting Residence this past year and is now planting a church in Marietta, GA. We hope to learn from Mike and also be a blessing to his church and as they launch later this fall. You can help them get word out about the plant by “liking” or sharing their social media: https://www.facebook.com/ImageChurchATL

Mike graduated with a degree Communication from The University of North Carolina where he also played baseball. He received his Masters of Divinity from Southeastern Baptist Theological Seminary. Mike was on staff at The Summit Church, located in Raleigh/Durham NC under pastor JD Greear from 2012-2017. He served as one of the pastors there and played a role in launching their Chapel Hill Campus. Mike, his wife Ashley and their daughter Adalyn Grace were sent out from the Summit in the Summer of 2017 to plant Image Church.
